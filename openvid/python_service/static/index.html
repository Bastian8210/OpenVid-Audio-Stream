Connection options

  • Wi-Fi: Ensure your PC and iPhone are on the same network.
  • Wired: Connect the iPhone by USB and enable Personal Hotspot for a direct, low-latency link.

Audio tips

  • Use the loopback source to stream system audio (default).
  • Lower blocksize for less latency; increase if you hear glitches.
  • Keep the screen awake to avoid iOS suspending audio playback.
